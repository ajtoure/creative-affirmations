ABOUT iAFFIRM

iAffirm is a mini project by Assiyah Jamilla Touré.

The app is inspired in great part by the esoteric self help book 'Creative Visualization' by Shakti Gawain.

These affirmations are meant to be read aloud for a time until they feel true and you can proceed to generate another.

I hope these affirmations are of comfort to you and that this app brings you a little bit of peace today!
